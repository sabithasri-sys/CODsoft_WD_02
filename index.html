About Us

Chaat Heaven is a vibrant food spot where the rich flavors of Indian street food come alive. Offering a delightful array of chaats, this place serves up crispy, tangy, and spicy treats like papdi chaat, bhel puri, and aloo tikki. Each dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and traditional spices, creating a burst of taste that transports you straight to the bustling streets of India.
